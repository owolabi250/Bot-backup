Two Factor Authentication

Two factor authentication is currently disabled, Would you like to enable two-factor authentication for your BotSchedule account? Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account by requiring a code in addition to your password when logging in. This reduces the risk of unauthorized access and helps to protect your personal information. Enabling two-factor authentication is strongly recommended to ensure the security of your account.

Set up Two Factor Auth

To enable two-factor authentication for your account, please follow these steps:

  • Log on to the Twilio website and sign up for an account.
  • Obtain your API key from Twilio.
  • Save your API key in your BotSchedule profile.
  • Enable two-factor authentication in your BotSchedule profile.
  • Log out of your BotSchedule account and log back in.

Rest assured that your data is safe and strictly personalized.
