Automation

At Botschedule, we are committed to providing an engaging self customized and fully automated learning experience for our users. Our platform offers a range of subject areas to choose from, and once you select your preferred subject, you will be automatically enrolled in a 30-60 days challenge, depending on the subject area.

As part of the challenge, you will have access to relevant topics in your chosen subject area, as well as a wealth of resources to help you gain knowledge and improve your skills. Our daily quizzes will test your understanding of each topic area, and a productivity graph will help you visualize your progress based on the average score earned from each quiz.

With our fully automated learning experience, you can focus on learning and improving, while we take care of the rest.
